About Us

From the party DJ to the lover of vinyl, Cat Scratchers has been providing pro audio gear to the Louisville area since 1972. Bob Katz started Cat Scratchers with little more than a dream and $10 in his pocket. Operating out of his garage, he won Best Hot New Business in 1975. Even back then, the personal touch was critical to his success.
